About us

inEnglish opened its doors in the summer of 2006. It arose from the dream of two teachers who wished to showcase the English language in a creative and innovative way, which is centred on the students’ needs and expectations. Bearing in mind that both the world of work and our personal lives make it necessary to keep increasingly closer contact with the English language, Nélia and Paula sought to understand the existing needs so as to draw up an innovative and efficient plan.

At inEnglish we focus on the student and on the goal they wish to reach. We provide a wide range of services which include not only teaching English, but also other services such as translation, aiming to be highly efficient and competitive, without neglecting the easy-going and family-oriented environment which we are known for.

InEnglish is a training entity which is recognised by DGERT (Direção-Geral do Emprego e das Relações de Trabalho), where English is taught using personalized methodologies, which are flexible in terms of their structure, content and schedule in order to meet each student’s needs and guarantee their success and satisfaction. At inEnglish, it’s important that students feel comfortable and motivated – this is the only way they can fully take advantage of the English learning process.

In the field of translation, the fact that it is done by bilingual professionals ensures that we stand out for the fluency of the final texts, where not even the most innocent comma is ignored. In fact, our experience in different areas guarantees that each text – be it the most literary or the most technical – is taken on as a challenge which deserves our full attention, professionalism and confidentiality.
