inEnglish General

If you are looking to initiate or deepen your knowledge of the English language, the inEnglish General course is the answer. With the aim of increasing communicative fluency and confidence when using the language, in this course we cover everyday life themes, grammar and structures which are adapted to real life situations and vocabulary that the students will use in a variety of contexts. The goal: to develop speaking, writing, listening and reading skills which will be useful in the real world.
The inEnglish General course is organised into seven levels, from Beginner (A1) to Upper-Advanced (C2) in accordance with the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages)

inEnglish Junior

The inEnglish Junior course is prepared to spark the interest of a child or teenager to learn English. When a first contact with the English language is encouraged at an early age and is maintained in a natural and progressive way, learning takes place almost without even realising it. The student learns by hearing, speaking and assimilating the language because they understand this is how we communicate at inEnglish. The goal: to develop linguistic skills which will be useful in the future which lies ahead…

Courses
The students are grouped by level and, whenever possible, by age group.
Between the age of 5 and 10 we recognise the importance of a dynamic methodology through which the student can take the first steps in terms of vocabulary, grammar and speaking by combining more traditional exercises with playful activities. Most importantly, at this stage it’s necessary to stimulate interest in learning English. For these ages, the course is planned in weekly classes with the duration of one hour.
Between the age of 11 and 18 the course is adapted to new needs and new ways of learning. The top priority becomes to develop speaking, writing, listening and reading skills so that the student can progress smoothly in their knowledge of the English language. For these ages, the course is planned in weekly classes with the duration of two hours and the books adopted focus on the international exams held by the University of Cambridge.

Our teachers know that a student who is motivated will not only learn but also grow in their love of the language. The modules follow a student’s book which is enriched with additional resources.

Tutorials
If you are looking for a more personalised approach and one which is specifically centred around the student’s academic needs, the tutorials may be a good option. Guided by an attentive teacher, the student gradually improves their understanding and performance regarding the contents which are covered in their English classes.

inEnglish Business & Careers 

For professionals who are looking to initiate or deepen their knowledge of the English language regarding a specific professional field so as to increase communicative fluency and confidence when using the language in that context, the inEnglish Business & Careers course is the answer. The goal: to improve the student’s professional performance by providing them with knowledge of English which is tailored to their functions.

+ info

We believe that, in an increasingly competitive world, each professional requires the right skills to work in an efficient and differentiating manner. inEnglish Exam Preparation

Pretende realizar um exame da Universidade de Cambridge? O curso inEnglish Cambridge Exam Preparation é a solução. Com uma elevada taxa de sucesso, o inEnglish é um centro de preparação reconhecido oficialmente pela Universidade de Cambridge. 

Objectivo: preparar o aluno para realizar um exame com sucesso e assim obter certificação reconhecida internacionalmente.

+ info

We believe that the University of Cambridge examinations are a valuable tool to validate the work carried out by the student throughout their English learning process and should be seen as an ambitious and motivating objective. The inEnglish Exam Preparation course is planned around each exam, thus systematically practising all of the skills which will be evaluated in that exam. This is the solution for those who wish to certify their English skills, which opens doors both academically as well as professionally.

Main examinations held by the University of Cambridge:
- Cambridge English: Young Learners (YLEs)
- Cambridge English: Key (KET)
- Cambridge English: Preliminary (PET)
- Cambridge English: First (FCE)
- Cambridge English: Advanced (CAE)
- Cambridge English: Proficiency (CPE)
- IELTS: International English Language Testing System
