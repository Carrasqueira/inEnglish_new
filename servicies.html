inEnglish Translation & Revision

inEnglish Translations

If quality and professionalism are important in your work, you will certainly demand the same level of quality and professionalism in documents that require a translation from English or Portuguese.
At inEnglish we provide all types of translation services, including those that involve documents that have to be officially certified (certified translations). The translations are carried out by bilingual translators and are subject to careful and thorough revisions. 

inEnglish Revision

If quality and professionalism are important in your work, you may need to ensure the consistency and cohesion of the texts you write. For that reason, at inEnglish we provide the service of text revision, be it of an academic nature or specifically related to a professional area.

inEnglish Companies

Each company has its own specific needs which are related to the sector or area in which it operates. At inEnglish we define a program which is tailored to the company’s needs in order to assure that we meet the employees’ requirements to perform their functions effectively and with the highest quality.

The training sessions may occur at our facilities or at the company, with the duration established according to the objectives outlined. 

inEnglish Preschool

inEnglish works together with several entities of the pre-school sector in order to promote the younger children’s first contact with the English language.

The sessions are held at the preschool facilities and are planned according to the day-to-day themes of this age group. By using the most varied playful activities, the children will assimilate their first words and become familiar with the pronunciation. This way, the first steps are taken so that learning English can be done in a natural and fun way.
